About Us

Sign Language and Hand Gestures Using CNN is a cutting-edge website that employs Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to recognize and interpret sign language and hand gestures. Our mission is to bridge communication gaps and empower individuals with hearing impairments by providing an intuitive and accessible communication platform.

Leveraging computer vision techniques, our system captures and analyzes hand movements with precision, allowing for accurate translation of sign language alphabets and gestures into text or speech. This technology revolutionizes communication for both those who use sign language and those who are not familiar with it.

Our user-friendly interface offers real-time sign language recognition through webcam or camera-enabled devices. This technology-driven platform facilitates seamless interaction and fosters a deeper understanding between diverse communities.

With a commitment to advancing sign language recognition and promoting inclusivity, we invite you to join us in making communication accessible to all. Together, we can create a world where technology bridges the gap and empowers individuals with hearing impairments to express themselves effortlessly.

Empower. Communicate. Inclusivity.
